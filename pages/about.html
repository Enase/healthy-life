Meet our team

Explore the faces behind our vibrant community - meet the organizers, the heartbeat of our collective journey towards a healthier life. Each of them brings a unique perspective, a wealth of experience, and an unwavering commitment to fostering a supportive and thriving community. Get ready to be inspired by the stories of those who drive our mission forward.

Samantha Lou

Samantha is a certified wellness coach and motivational speaker, dedicated to guiding individuals towards a life of fulfillment and vitality. Drawing from her own transformative journey, she empowers others to overcome challenges, prioritize self-care, and cultivate a positive mindset.

Outside of coaching sessions, Samantha enjoys writing, hiking, and fostering a community of support. Join Samantha in unlocking your true potential and embracing a holistic approach to well-being.

Jerald Mild

Jerald is a passionate fitness enthusiast who believes in the transformative power of a healthy lifestyle. With a background in nutrition and personal training, he empowers others to achieve their fitness goals and embrace a balanced life. Jerald's energetic personality and commitment to well-being make him a valuable member of our community.

Outside the gym, Jerald enjoys exploring new hiking trails, experimenting with nutritious recipes, and sharing his knowledge with fellow health enthusiasts. Join Jerald on the journey to a healthier, more vibrant life!

Diana Koval

Diana is a dedicated yoga instructor and mindfulness advocate committed to helping individuals find balance in their hectic lives. With a soothing presence and expertise in holistic wellness, she guides our community members through practices that nurture both the body and mind.

Outside the studio, Diana is a nature lover who finds inspiration in the beauty of the outdoors. Join Diana in exploring the profound benefits of yoga and mindfulness on your journey to a calmer and more centered self.

Zoui Karlsberg

Zoui is a seasoned nutritionist and advocate for sustainable living. With a focus on plant-based nutrition and mindful eating, she inspires our community to make informed choices for their health and the well-being of the planet.

Outside of her nutrition practice, Zoui enjoys gardening, promoting eco-friendly practices, and sharing delicious plant-powered recipes. Connect with Zoui to discover how small changes in your diet can lead to a healthier, more sustainable lifestyle.

Thank you for getting to know the driving forces behind our community. These individuals embody the spirit of health and well-being, shaping the very essence of our collective pursuit.
