Meet our team

Welcome to our community dedicated to embracing and enhancing the most precious gift we have - life. At the core of our beliefs is the understanding that a healthy life is a fulfilling life. Join us on the journey to discover how simple changes in lifestyle can lead to profound improvements in our well-being. Together, let's make every moment count by prioritizing health and happiness.

Our support group is open to everyone. We welcome any donations, but our meetings are absolutely free, except for fitness sessions.

We meet at Civic Hall in Slaithwaite. 25 Cartoon Street, Slaithwaite, HD7 5EH
